About

My name is Sam and I'm a freelance front-end web developer with a strong focus on interfaces and experiences working remotely from London.

My outlook on life is that there is always a problem to be solved and these can be achieved through a combination of creativity and logic. Each problem that I overcome is a learning experience for me which informs the project I am working on.

My focus is mobile-first responsive websites using HTML and Javacript and I have a yearning desire to experiment and learn.
